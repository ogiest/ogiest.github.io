Quick Troubleshooting FAQ

Q: How do I access the site through the Unstoppable Domain (quran-web3.nft)?

A: You can use Web3-native browsers like Brave or Opera which support blockchain domains automatically. Alternatively, you can use standard browsers by adding .limo or .to at the end (e.g., quran-web3.nft.limo) to load it via an IPFS gateway.

Q: Do I need a crypto wallet or paid gas fees to read this blog?

A: Absolutely not. This project is fully open, educational, and free. You do not need to connect a wallet, pay gas fees, or own any cryptocurrency to access our content.
