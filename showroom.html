Level 4: Interactive Components (Alpine.js)

These components utilize the Alpine.js plugin built into your bundle.js. Perfect for interactive Tajweed quizzes or FAQ accordions.

1. Folding Accordion (FAQ / Technical Details)

The .nft domain purchased from Unstoppable Domains is owned permanently with no annual renewal fees. It is stored directly in the owner's crypto wallet, making it censorship-resistant and fully integrated into the IPFS network.
