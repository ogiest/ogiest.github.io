Level 4: Interactive Components (Alpine.js - Complex)

These components utilize the Alpine.js plugin included in the bundle.js file. Perfect for interactive quizzes or FAQ accordions.

1. Collapsible Accordion (FAQ / Technical Details)

The .nft domain purchased from Unstoppable Domains implies perpetual ownership with no annual renewal fees. It is stored directly in the owner's crypto wallet, making it censorship-resistant and fully integrated with the IPFS network. 
<div x-data="{ open: false }" class="hh sm _g ch pm sg ci ac">
    <button @click="open = !open" class="vd tc yf wf ph sj wj fk kk wm">
        <span>Question Click Here?</span>
        <span x-text="open ? '−' : '+'" class="vj"></span>
    </button>
    <div x-show="open" x-transition class="bh ch pm kc lc dk pk">
        Answer or hidden text details go here.
    </div>
</div>

2. Mini Interactive Quiz (Public Education / Tajweed Quiz)

Quick Tajweed Quiz

What is the tajweed rule when a Nun Sukun meets the letter Mim (م)?

🎉 Correct Answer (Idgham Bighunnah)!
❌ Incorrect, try studying it again!

When Nun Sukun meets Mim, the sound of Nun is merged into the letter Mim accompanied by a nasal sound (ghunnah).

    </div>
